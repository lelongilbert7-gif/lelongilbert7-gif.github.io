An end-to-end medallion analytics platform built on Microsoft Fabric — ingesting US flight records, airline dimensions, and live weather data through a Bronze → Silver → Gold architecture into a Direct Lake semantic model.

KEY FINDING

Rainy days (>5mm precipitation) increase average departure delays by 63% — from 13.8 min to 22.5 min — quantified by independently joining BTS flight records with Open-Meteo weather data across 15 US airports.
